Seshat

The name "Seshat" is inspired by the ancient Egyptian goddess Seshat, who was known as the goddess of writing, measurement, and wisdom in Egyptian mythology. In Egyptian mythology, Seshat was often depicted with a notepad and a stylus, symbolizing her role in recording and documenting important information. In a similar vein, my application helps you organize and sort your files, ensuring that your digital documents and data are well-organized and easily accessible.
